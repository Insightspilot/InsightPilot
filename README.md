# Insight Pilot

🚀 **Cloud-native Business Intelligence platform with zero setup.**

Create an organization, connect your data sources, build dashboards, and share insights across your team in minutes.

Insight Pilot is an open-source analytics platform designed to make data exploration and dashboard creation accessible to everyone—without vendor lock-in or expensive licensing.

---

## ✨ Features

* 🔌 Connect your own databases and data sources
* 📊 Build interactive dashboards
* 🏢 Organization-based workspace management
* 🤝 Share dashboards across teams
* 🔒 Data remains under your control
* ⚡ Modern cloud-native architecture
* 🌐 Self-hosted and open-source
* 🎯 Designed for fast onboarding and minimal setup

---

## 🏗️ Architecture

Insight Pilot follows a modern full-stack architecture:

### Frontend

* Next.js
* TypeScript
* React

### Backend

* Python
* REST APIs
* Scalable service-oriented design

### Data Ownership

Unlike many analytics platforms, Insight Pilot does **not own or store your business data**.

Users connect their own databases directly to the platform. Insight Pilot only accesses the data required for querying and visualization, allowing organizations to maintain ownership and control of their data infrastructure.
